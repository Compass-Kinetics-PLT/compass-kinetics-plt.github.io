👤 Founders' Profile - Compass Kinetics PLT

Chong leads the firm’s strategic and coaching direction, combining business acumen with leadership development expertise to help clients achieve clarity and sustainable growth. Chai Keok complements this with her strength in people engagement, operational excellence, and mindful leadership, ensuring every initiative delivers both impact and authenticity. Together, they uphold Compass Kinetics’ core philosophy — Awaken, Align, Appreciate, and Release — guiding individuals and organizations to navigate change with purpose and confidence.
