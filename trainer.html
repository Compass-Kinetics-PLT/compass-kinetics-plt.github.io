🌿 Trainer Profile

Ooi Chong Kheng is the Co-founder and Principal Coach of Compass Kinetics PLT, with over 30 years of experience in the semiconductor industry, including his role as Design Verification Engineering Manager and Intel Leadership Coach. A Certified Scrum Master and Coach, Chong Kheng holds a First-Class Honours degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Malaya and an MBA. His work integrates structured learning, experiential training, and mindset coaching through the A.N.G. and N.O.W. models. Grounded in Buddhist principles, he brings presence and strategic clarity to leadership transformation.
